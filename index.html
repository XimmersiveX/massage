The Art and Science of Massage

Just as music depends on the acoustical universe, massage depends upon the wondrous structures and function of anatomy and physiology. Through years of study and training we gain an understanding of how the muscles in the body work together and the function of each. With our scientific knowlage we can target the muscles that need focused in order to releave pain and stress.

The difference in a good massage and a great massage is when the therapist is able to synthesize all the elements of evaluation, technique, and method into an intuitive healing dance. Deep Tissue

Deep tissue massage uses more pressure than a therapeutic massage. It can help relieve tight muscles, chronic muscle pain, and anxiety.

Therapeutic

A therapeutic massage is used to help fully relax the body and release muscle knots.

Sounds and Aroma

During your massage, sounds and aroma are utilized to aid in the calming and soothing properties of a well exicuted massage.
